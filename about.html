How I came to grant advisory

Before founding BizGrants Consulting in 2023, I spent years in roles where I saw firsthand how much workforce funding was left unclaimed by Singapore employers — not because the schemes weren’t available, but because the documentation, role redesign, and OJT planning required to secure them sat outside what most HR teams had bandwidth for. The funding existed; the bridge to it didn’t.

That gap became the work. BizGrants Consulting was set up to be a single point of contact for SMEs who want to hire mid-career talent, redesign roles, or upskill existing staff — and need someone to translate business plans into the language Workforce Singapore programme partners can approve.

What I work on

My engagements typically cover four areas:

  • CCP scoping & pathway selection — mapping a hiring or reskilling plan to the right Workforce Singapore programme partner and pathway.
  • Job redesign & OJT plan drafting — translating responsibilities into assessor-ready job descriptions, OJT activities, KPIs, and milestones.
  • Documentation & submission — preparing the application pack (ACRA, salary evidence, CPF statements, undertakings) and managing programme-partner clarifications.
  • Claims support — structuring the post-approval evidence and claim submissions so the salary support actually flows through to payroll.

How I work

Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute scoping call. If there’s a real fit — meaning the role, the candidate profile, and the company structure all line up against an existing CCP pathway — we move to a written engagement letter on a success-fee model. You don’t pay until the application is approved.

I keep client lists deliberately small because every CCP application is bespoke. The work is structured: clear documentation, weekly progress notes, and direct access to me throughout. No agency layer, no junior associates passing your file around.
