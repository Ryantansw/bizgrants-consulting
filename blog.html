Frequently Asked Questions about CCP, Job Redesign & Government Grants

What is the difference between CCP and Job Redesign Grant?

The CCP supports structured reskilling while Job Redesign supports changes to job scope and processes.

Can my company apply for CCP for digital or sustainability roles?

Yes. There are CCP pathways for digital, marketing, HR and sustainability. You can check eligible roles here.

How long does a CCP application take?

Two to six weeks depending on pathway and documentation.

Is BizGrants Consulting success fee based?

Yes. You only pay if your application is approved.
