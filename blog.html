Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between CCP and Job Redesign Grant?

The CCP focuses on reskilling individuals, while Job Redesign supports changes in job scope and workflow for productivity.

Can my company apply for CCP?

Yes. CCP pathways exist for digital, marketing, HR, sustainability and more.

How long does CCP approval take?

Typically 2–6 weeks depending on pathway and documentation completeness.

Is BizGrants success-fee based?

Yes. You only pay if the application gets approved.
