Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between CCP and Job Redesign Grant?

The CCP focuses on reskilling individuals into new or substantially changed roles, while Job Redesign supports changes in job scope and workflow for productivity and transformation.

Can my company apply for CCP?

Yes, there are CCP pathways for digital, marketing, HR, sustainability and more. The key is a clear skills or scope conversion and a structured OJT plan.

How long does CCP approval take?

Typically 2 to 6 weeks depending on pathway, document readiness and programme partner workload.

Is BizGrants success-fee based?

Yes, you only pay if the application gets approved, which keeps it low risk for employers.
