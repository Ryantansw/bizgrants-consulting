Frequently Asked Questions about CCP, Job Redesign & Government Grants

What is the difference between CCP and Job Redesign Grant?

The CCP supports structured reskilling of mid-career or existing staff, while the Job Redesign Grant supports changes in job scope, processes, or technology adoption. Many companies combine both for maximum salary support. See supported roles.

Can my company apply for CCP for digital, marketing, or sustainability roles?

Yes, there are CCP pathways for digital marketing, tech, HR, sustainability, and more. Check eligible roles or contact us for assessment.

How long does a CCP application take?

Typically, 2–6 weeks from submission to approval, depending on documentation and pathway. We advise and manage the process end-to-end.
