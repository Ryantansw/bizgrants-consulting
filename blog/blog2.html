Why Our Success-Based Consulting Model Benefits You

Applying for government funding like CCP can be complex and time-consuming. At BizGrants Consulting, we offer a success-based consulting model: we only charge our flat fee after your CCP application is approved. If the application does not go through, there is no fee. This "no win, no fee" approach aligns our interests with yours — we are motivated to secure funding for you.

This model is different from traditional consultants who often require upfront or ongoing fees regardless of outcome. By working with us, you minimize financial risk and ensure that our efforts directly contribute to your success. Our consultants are highly experienced in CCP applications and will handle all the paperwork and coordination for you.

During the process, we maintain clear communication. You will know exactly what we are doing at each step, from identifying the best CCP pathway to submitting forms and following up with the authorities. Because our fee depends on your success, we strive for efficiency and thoroughness.

In summary, BizGrants Consulting’s success-based fee model means you get expert CCP application support with no upfront cost. We only succeed when you succeed. Contact us today to learn how we can help your company take advantage of Career Conversion Programme funding.
