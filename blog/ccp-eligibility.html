What is the Career Conversion Programme (CCP) and Who is Eligible?

Last updated: June 2025

Understanding the CCP

The Career Conversion Programme (CCP) is Workforce Singapore’s flagship initiative to help companies reskill mid-career hires or existing staff for new or redesigned roles. For many Singapore employers, this scheme translates to substantial salary support, enabling business transformation while minimising risk.

Who is Eligible?

  • For Companies: Registered and operating in Singapore, including both SMEs and non-SMEs. SMEs enjoy enhanced support rates.
  • For Employees: Singapore Citizens or Permanent Residents, aged 21 and above, either newly hired (within the last 3 months) or existing staff whose jobs are redesigned (with at least 50% change in job content).
  • Employees must not be company shareholders, immediate family of shareholders, or ex-employees rejoining within 6 months.

Why Consider CCP?

  • Access up to 90% salary support for reskilled staff (3 to 6 months, depending on pathway).
  • Strengthen workforce capabilities and stay competitive amid rapid change.
  • Mitigate hiring and training risks with structured support.
