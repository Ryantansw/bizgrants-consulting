The true cost of CCP: flat fee, success based and traditional consulting

A common question from Singapore SMEs is, “How much does CCP consulting cost?” The answer depends on the fee model used. Below is a simple comparison of typical models and why BizGrants chose a flat fee, success linked approach.

Comparing CCP consulting models

Model How it works Pros Cons
Traditional hourly or retainer Pay per hour or per month regardless of outcome Deep engagement; may support broader HR or transformation work High upfront cost and no guarantee of CCP approval; harder for SMEs to budget
Pure success fee Pay a % of approved grant amount only if funded No upfront risk; advisor is motivated to secure approval Percentages can be high; some advisors select only “easy” cases
Flat fee with approval linked component (BizGrants) Fixed project fee, with a portion collected only upon approval Transparent and capped cost; risk sharing; suitable for complex cases Works best for employers willing to commit time and documents

Why BizGrants uses a flat fee model with success link

  • Costs are clear from the start, no surprise charges
  • You only commit the second tranche when the CCP is approved
  • We can take on more complex cases, not just low hanging fruit
  • We focus on quality documentation, OJT and advisory, not just quick volume

What you receive in a CCP advisory project

  • Eligibility review and grant mapping
  • Role redesign and job description drafting
  • OJT planning aligned to SkillsFuture frameworks
  • Application form completion and clarifications
  • Support on claims preparation and evidence tracking

FAQ on flat fee CCP consulting

  • Q: What if my CCP is not approved?
    A: You do not pay the second tranche, and we will advise on what can be improved for a future attempt.
  • Q: Are there hidden charges?
    A: No. All charges are stated clearly in your service agreement.
  • Q: How do I start?
    A: Book a free eligibility call and we will propose a fee and scope before you commit.
