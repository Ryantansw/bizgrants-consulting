The True Cost of CCP: Flat-Fee, Success-Based vs. Traditional Consulting

One question we hear often from Singapore SMEs: “How much does it cost to get help with CCP grants?” The reality is, not all consulting models are created equal. Here, we break down the cost structures—and explain why BizGrants Consulting’s flat-fee, success-only approach is designed to protect employers and remove risk.

Comparing Consulting Models for CCP and Grant Advisory

Model How It Works Pros Cons
Traditional (Hourly/Retainer) Pay upfront or monthly regardless of outcome Long-term relationship; may include HR transformation High upfront cost; no guarantee of approval; hard for SMEs to budget
Commission-Based (Success Fee Only) Pay a % of grant amount only if approved No risk to employer; consultant is incentivised for approval Percentages can be high (15–25% of grant); may push for only “easy” cases
Flat Fee, Success-Only (BizGrants Consulting) Pay a fixed fee, in two tranches: 50% on project start, 50% only if approved. Full refund if not approved. Transparent; capped cost; risk-free; high service levels for all clients Best suited for companies ready to commit to the process and documentation

Why BizGrants Consulting’s Model Works for Singapore Employers

  • Pay only if your CCP application succeeds—if not, you get a full refund of your second tranche
  • Clear pricing from the start, no hidden charges
  • Shared risk: our team is motivated to get your application approved
  • Full project management—eligibility, OJT plan, clarifications, and claims support

Our Promise: Transparent, Aligned, and Risk-Free

  • We only take on projects with a high chance of success (after free eligibility check)
  • You are kept informed at every stage, with clear deliverables
