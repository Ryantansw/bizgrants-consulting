Regional digital reskilling at a boutique beauty and aesthetics brand

Case / 2024−14 Beauty & aesthetics / specialty retail Single reskill, anchored to regional launches

A marketing executive at a boutique skincare and aesthetics brand in Singapore, reskilled into a digital-marketing-and-analytics specialist as part of a broader regional expansion play, with OJT anchored to specific market launches rather than abstract competency uplift.

Industry context

The client operated a boutique skincare and aesthetics brand expanding from a single-market presence into a multi-market regional footprint. The expansion required digital-marketing capabilities the company did not previously rely on: performance marketing tied to specific market entries, regional analytics tooling, content localisation across multiple Southeast Asian markets. The existing marketing executive had run a generalist scope, with limited exposure to those specific competencies.

What changed and why CCP applied

The redesigned role required new competencies in performance marketing, analytics tooling, and regional content localisation that were absent from the incumbent's original scope. The redesign was substantive enough to qualify for CCP reskilling: more than half of the daily work would change, and the new responsibilities required structured training to execute credibly.

Our strategy

We framed the redesign around the regional expansion plan, anchoring the OJT modules to specific market launches so that the structured training had clear business-outcome milestones rather than abstract competency uplift. Each major OJT milestone tied back to a measurable launch deliverable: a regional landing-page test, a market-specific paid acquisition campaign, a localised content programme. This made both the candidate's progress and the business case visible week by week.

OJT activities included

  • Drafting a redesigned digital-marketing-and-analytics Job Description with measurable scope expansion against the incumbent's original generalist role.
  • Building an OJT plan tied to specific regional launch milestones, with each module producing a measurable launch artefact.
  • Producing the reskilling submission and trainee detail documentation aligned to the relevant programme partner.

Outcome

Role covered
Marketing executive reskilled into digital marketing and analytics specialist
Approach
Reskilling with OJT anchored to specific regional market launches
Status
Reskilling application progressed and was approved
Strategic value
Client supported its regional digital expansion with funded structured training covering the redesign period, with each OJT milestone aligned to a launch deliverable

Anchoring OJT milestones to commercial launch deliverables is a small framing decision with outsized impact. Assessors find the case more credible because the training has visible business outcomes; the candidate ramps faster because the structure forces real work, not theoretical exercises; and the company gets immediate value back during the funded period rather than waiting until afterwards.
