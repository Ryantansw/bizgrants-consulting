CCP Grant in Singapore, how it works

A plain English primer for HR and business owners. We explain eligibility, what the grant supports, documents you need, and how to plan timelines and evidence.

Check eligibility

Key concepts

Eligibility

Singapore registered employer, redesigned role, and structured OJT plan with clear activities and milestones.

Supported items

Salary support during structured OJT and, where relevant, approved external training. We align your plan to pathway requirements.

Evidence

OJT log, supervisor sign off, training records and payroll proof. We provide trackers, formats and examples.

Documents checklist

  • Job description and redesigned scope
  • OJT plan with activities, hours and outcomes
  • Employment contract, salary proof, CPF statements
  • Company particulars and ACRA
  • Undertakings and declarations
