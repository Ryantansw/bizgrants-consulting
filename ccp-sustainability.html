Example OJT themes

Materiality and governance

Identify key environmental and social topics, define boundaries and set up data governance for consistent reporting.

Baselining and measurement

Collect baseline data for energy, water, waste or emissions, and define KPIs to track improvements.

Policy and process design

Draft procurement, waste or operational policies, and pilot targeted improvements with measurable outcomes.

Reporting and disclosure

Compile progress updates and prepare management ready briefs for annual disclosures.

Stakeholder training

Prepare bite sized guides and coach teams to adopt better practices across functions.
