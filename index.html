What are Career Conversion Programmes?

Career Conversion Programmes (CCPs) are Workforce Singapore-funded initiatives designed to help companies reskill mid-career professionals or upskill existing staff into new or redesigned roles.

Funding & Benefits:

  • Receive up to 70%–90% salary support for eligible staff undergoing structured OJT and reskilling.
  • Reduce hiring and training costs while building a future-ready workforce.
  • Tap into a wide talent pool for new growth, digital, or sustainability roles.
  • Benefit from expert grant advisory and application support, making transformation easy for any business size.
With over 100 CCPs available, companies in Singapore can adapt, grow, and innovate with confidence—knowing that key roles are government-supported.
