What are Career Conversion Programmes & Job Redesign Grants?

Career Conversion Programmes (CCPs) and Job Redesign Grants are Workforce Singapore and SkillsFuture-funded initiatives designed to help companies reskill or upskill staff, hire mid-career professionals, or redesign existing jobs for digital transformation and business growth.

Funding & Benefits:

  • Receive up to 70%–90% salary support for eligible staff undergoing structured OJT, reskilling, or job redesign.
  • Reduce hiring and training costs while building a future-ready workforce.
  • Tap into a wide talent pool for new growth, digital, sustainability, and tech roles.
  • Benefit from expert grant advisory and application support, making transformation easy for any business size.
With over 100 CCPs and SkillsFuture pathways available, companies in Singapore can adapt, grow, and innovate with confidence, knowing that key roles are government-supported.

Who Is Eligible for CCP & Job Redesign Grants?

  • Singapore-registered companies hiring or reskilling Singaporeans/PRs into new or redesigned roles
  • Roles must involve at least 50% change in job content for job redesign funding
  • Open to SMEs and larger employers; enhanced funding for SMEs
  • Staff must not be shareholders or family members of directors
  • Book a free eligibility check or see our role examples

Frequently Asked Questions (CCP, Job Redesign & SkillsFuture Grants)

Who is eligible for the Career Conversion Programme (CCP)?

Most Singapore-registered companies hiring or reskilling Singapore Citizens or PRs into new or redesigned roles are eligible for CCP funding. Book a free eligibility check with us.

How much salary support can I get?

Up to 90% salary support for new hires, job redesign, or structured reskilling through CCP, Job Redesign Grant, or SkillsFuture Singapore initiatives.

What roles can be supported?

Roles in digital, sustainability, HR, tech, marketing, business operations, and more may qualify. See our Services page for examples.
