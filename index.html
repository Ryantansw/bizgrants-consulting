What are Career Conversion Programmes and Job Redesign Grants?

Career Conversion Programmes and Job Redesign Grants are Workforce Singapore and SkillsFuture-funded initiatives designed to help companies reskill or upskill staff, hire mid-career professionals, or redesign existing jobs for digital transformation and business growth.

  • Receive up to 70 percent to 90 percent salary support for eligible staff undergoing structured OJT, reskilling, or job redesign.
  • Reduce hiring and training costs while building a future-ready workforce.
  • Tap into a wide talent pool for new growth, digital, sustainability, and tech roles.
  • Benefit from expert grant advisory and application support, suitable for SMEs and larger companies.

Read our blog for eligibility tips, case studies, and CCP success stories.

Who Is Eligible for CCP and Job Redesign Grants?

  • Singapore-registered companies hiring or reskilling Singaporeans or PRs into new or redesigned roles
  • Roles must involve at least 50 percent change in job content for job redesign funding
  • Open to SMEs and larger employers; enhanced funding for SMEs
  • Staff must not be shareholders or family members of directors
  • Book a free eligibility check or see our role examples
