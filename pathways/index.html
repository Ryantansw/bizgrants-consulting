CCP Pathways in Singapore: Eligible Roles & Funding Support by Sector

A reference guide to common CCP pathways in Singapore, including role examples and job families commonly used during role scoping discussions. Roles indicated below are non-exhaustive. Get in touch for a complimentary assessment.

1

Different job scope

Substantial change in responsibilities or operating context.

Eligible staffNew hires only
Min. salary$3,500 (some cases $3,000)
Support duration3 months
Common job families and example titles

Common job families

Leadership and management Sales and marketing Business development Project management Operations and administration

  • Operations Executive / Operations Manager
  • Project Coordinator / Project Executive
  • Business Development Executive / Manager
  • Account Executive / Sales Executive
  • Admin and Operations Executive (with process ownership)

3

Overseas markets

Overseas postings and market expansion with in-market execution responsibilities.

Eligible staffNew and existing
Min. salary$4,000
Support durationUp to 9 months
Typical role archetypes

Role archetypes

Regional business development manager Country manager Regional partnerships manager Regional operations manager Regional sales manager International expansion lead

Typical proof points

  • In-market responsibilities, not only coordination from Singapore
  • Country-specific commercial execution and stakeholder management
  • Expansion plan translated into measurable tasks
  • Skills gap tied to the target market’s operating context
