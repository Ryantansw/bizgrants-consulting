Our CCP Consulting Services

Explore how BizGrants supports your team in securing Workforce Singapore (WSG)'s Career Conversion Programme (CCP) funding – from job redesign to full documentation.

Career Reskilling Solutions for Companies

We assist employers in reskilling employees through structured CCP pathways aligned to WSG’s priorities. You get access to up to 90% salary support – we handle the entire submission process on your behalf.

  • ✔ Roles in Sustainability, Green Transformation, and ESG: For businesses seeking to build internal capabilities in sustainability, including environmental reporting, sustainable procurement, and green operations.
  • ✔ Roles in Strategic HR and Human Capital Transformation: For HR teams taking on workforce planning, talent development, or digitalisation of HR practices aligned with business strategy.
  • ✔ Roles in Digital Advertising, Branding, and Performance Marketing: For companies aiming to build or strengthen their digital presence through social media, SEO/SEM, or online advertising capabilities.
  • ✔ Overseas Deployment for Market Expansion and Business Development: For Singapore-based employees taking on responsibilities in new international markets through on-the-ground deployment or remote market activation.
  • ✔ Roles Driving Operational Efficiency and Process Transformation: For companies focused on automation, cost reduction, digital tools adoption, or lean workflow improvement.
  • ✔ Cross-functional Roles in SMEs: Designed for SME teams building multifunctional capabilities such as finance, admin, ops, marketing, or account management within the same headcount.
  • ✔ Roles for Mid-Career Hires Switching into New Functions: Supports companies onboarding professionals with adjacent but non-identical backgrounds, enabling them to pivot into your industry or function through structured OJT.
  • ✔ Roles in UI/UX, Creative Content, and Brand Identity: For firms ramping up design innovation — whether in digital interfaces, visual branding, marketing creatives, or product packaging.
  • ✔ Roles in Experience Design and Service Recovery (Tourism/Hospitality): Especially relevant for businesses redesigning their customer journey, digital offerings, or guest touchpoints in a post-pandemic environment.
  • ✔ Regional Sales, Distribution and Export Trade Roles: Supports capabilities in managing overseas distributors, regional clients, trade documentation, or cross-border supply chain operations.

Note: Depending on your business goals and industry, additional CCP pathways may be relevant. Engage us for a consultation and we’ll assess your eligibility and recommend the right approach.
