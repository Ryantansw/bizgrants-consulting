Our Key CCP Pathways

Reskill Sustainability & ESG Talent

Redesign roles or hire talent for ESG, green procurement, sustainable operations, and environmental reporting. We help you meet eligibility for green-focused CCPs.

Transform HR & Human Capital Functions

Redesign HR jobs for talent management, workforce planning, or digital HR transformation. Aligns with CCP for Human Capital roles.

Digital & Performance Marketing

Onboard or redesign digital marketers for paid media, CRM, analytics, social, or content. Supports CCP for Digital Advertising & Marketing Professionals.

Product Innovation & Design Thinking

Support creative, design, and UI/UX teams in adopting design-centric and product innovation skills. Links to CCP for Design Professionals.

Overseas Market Expansion

Redesign or rotate talent for international roles or business development—approved under CCP OMIP (Overseas Markets Immersion Programme).

SME Generalist Upskilling

Adapt to New roles in SMEs (e.g. sales/admin, ops/marketing hybrids) and secure funding through CCP for SME new hires.

Digital Professionals

Admin, sales & marketing, operations, project management, business development roles requiring Digital Proficiencies.

Tourism, Hospitality & Service Excellence

Redesign customer experience, MICE, or hospitality roles to qualify for CCP for Tourism Professionals. Supports guest recovery and digital journey.

Wholesale Trade & Export Ops

Reskill teams for cross-border trade, supply chain, procurement or export roles. Covers CCP for Wholesale Trade Professionals.

Note: Many more CCPs are available (ICT, F&B, Biomedical, etc). We match the most relevant programme to your business for maximum funding and best results.
